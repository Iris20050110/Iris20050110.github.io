Another Easy Breakfast Recipe!

I figured out an easy way to make banana pie!!
This idea came from the time when I stuck in my dorm and had online classes. The school refectory had almost nothing to eat because almost nobody had came back to campus. I brought some bananas and pieces of toasts from the refectory to the dorm. Cut the bananas into slices, mash them, and then put them into the toasts. A little tip was to make several small cuts on the toasts, so that it was easier to heat the mashed banana inside. Heat it in the microwave for 60 seconds!
The picture looks a little messy...BUT it tasted Good! I loved the wonderful smelling of bananas burrowing out of the toasts!
