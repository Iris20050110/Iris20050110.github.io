"A Novel Music Recommendation System using Music-Emotion Recognition Models"

In this study, I compare a emotion-based music recommendation system with a traditional style-and- artists-based music recommendation system adapted by most music streaming services nowadays. To quantize emotion as a measurable variable, I use the classic Russell's emotion ring that classifies 4 basic types of emotions--excitement, calm, depression, and anger--into a quadrant. After trainings 3 different machine learning models, the music-emotion recognizer is able to reach almost 80% accuracy F1 score. It demonstrates especial capability in predicting emotions excitement and anger.


"An Analysis on the Relationship between Leadership and Responsibility Aversion"

As leadership increasingly becomes critical for society today, we grow interest in the responsibility and possible responsibility aversion brought by leadership. We define responsibility aversion as the preference to minimize one's causal role in outcome generation. We hypothesize a causal relationship exists between leadership and responsibility aversion. The level of aversion is dependent on both the responsibilities of leaders and the common traits of leadership positions.
Through a survey towards high school and college students, we found that responsibility delegation is a common approach for leaders to avoid their taking account of their own responsibilities. Furthermore, leaders who understand that their decisions and attitudes would affect group morale and efficiency are more responsible for their groups.
