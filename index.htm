Braille Translator

I am now developing a Braile-to-English image translator. The general idea is to train a deep learning model to recognzie Braille characters in English letters, adapt an image scanning tool, and deploy it on a web. I will document my progress and improvment every week here. Click the Braille Blog section to check more details about my work!
Here is the link for my Braille Book Organization for the Blind! You can also check our prototype for the Braille-to-English translator!

Research

I am especially interested in the Interdisciplinary study of computing and psychology. In my new study, I built a machine learning model that predicts emotions in music using Russell's emotion quadrants. With my teammates, we conducted experiments on the psychological relationship between leadership and responsibility aversion. Currently, we are trying to find ways to use AI to deal with mental illness. Click the Research section to learn more about what I have explored!
